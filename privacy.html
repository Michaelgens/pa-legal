Privacy Policy — Personnal Assistant

Last updated: 2025-09-15

What we access

Personnal Assistant uses Google Sign-In to authenticate you and (only with your permission) reads events from your Google Calendar so the app can show and manage your schedule.

Data we read

  • Google profile info (name, email) — for account identification.
  • Google Calendar events — to display and manage your schedule in the app.

Where data is stored

Access tokens are used only to call Google APIs. If we store refresh tokens or user data it is stored encrypted on our backend (or: tokens are stored only on your device). Replace this line with your actual storage behavior.

How to remove access

To stop access, go to your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access, and remove MyApp; or sign out from within the app.
